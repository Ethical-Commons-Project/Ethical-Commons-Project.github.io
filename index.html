The Ethical Commons Project

Reclaiming the Digital Space for Humanity

In an era where our social interactions are increasingly mediated by digital platforms, we find ourselves asking: Who controls these spaces, and what are their priorities? All too often, the answer is corporations driven by profit, not the well-being of the people they serve.

The Ethical Commons Project is a movement to take back control. We aim to create open-source, non-profit digital services designed with a single purpose: bring humanity back to the internet.
